Controls To Play

  • Use Arrow Keys To Move Your Bomberman Hero.

  • Press Space Bar To Launch A Bomb.

  • Use 'P' Key Or 'ESC' Key To Pause The Game.


Explanation

  • To complete the levels, place bombs to kill enemies and destroy obstacles.

  • Exploding bombs can set off other bombs, kill or injure enemies, and destroy obstacles.

  • They can also kill or injure the player character and destroy powerups.
